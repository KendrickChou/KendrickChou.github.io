Jiahao Zhou 「周嘉豪」

I am a second year Ph.D. student at IPADS Lab, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

Under the guidance of Prof. Mingkai Dong and Prof. Haibo Chen, my research focuses on storage systems. Currently, I am working on improving the retrievability of data in file systems. Previously, I focused on enhancing the practicality of DNA storage systems.

Publication
